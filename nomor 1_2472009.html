About Bikini Bottom

Bikini Bottom is the vibrant underwater city where Spongebob Squarepants and his friemds live. From the Krusty Krab to Jellyfish Field, there's always something fun happening here!

Top Attractions

-Krusty Krab: Home of the famaous Krabby Patty. Don't forget to try one!

-Jellyfish Field: Perfect for jellyfish catching and pionios

-Goo Lagoon: A beautiful lagoon for swimming and sunbathing

-Sandy's Treehome: Visit Sandy Cheeks's treehome for a taste of Texas underware.

Travel Tips

-Bring your jellyfish net for a fun day at Jellyfish Fields.

-Don't forget to try a Krabby Patty at the Krusty Krab

- Be careful around Plankton's Chum Buckets-- its's not as tasty as it sound!

About the Author

SpongeBob SquarePants is a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and a proud resident of Bikini Bottom. He loves jellyfishing, bubble blowing, and sharing his adventures with the world.
